### Data Stores

#### Concepts

##### Data Persistence

* Persistent Data Store - durable, survives reboots, restarts, power cycles
    * Glacier
    * RDS
* Transient Data Store - temporarily stored, passed to another process or persistent store
    * SQS
    * SNS
* Ephemeral Data Store - data is lost when stopped
    * EC2 instance store
    * Memcached
